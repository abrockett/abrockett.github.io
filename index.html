Instructions:  The purpose of this form is to identify problems your hearing loss may be causing you. If you wear hearing aids, answer the questions according to how you communicate when the hearing aids are in use.

One of the five ratings on the right should be assigned to each of the statements below.

(1) Almost never (or never)

(2) Occasionally (about 1/4 of the time)

(3) About 1/2 the time

(4) Frequently (about 3/4 of the time)

(5) Practically always (or always)
